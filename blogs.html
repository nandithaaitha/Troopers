Explore Accessible Places in Dallas

Discover 15 famous places in Dallas that offer accessibility features for various disabilities. Plan your journey with ease and enjoy an inclusive experience.

Dallas Arboretum and Botanical Garden

Brief Description: A sprawling 66-acre garden that showcases seasonal plants, water features, and art installations.

Guidelines:
- Requirements: Comfortable walking shoes recommended; bring sun protection.
- Do’s: Stay on designated paths, enjoy quiet areas, and bring reusable water bottles.
- Don’ts: Do not pick flowers, avoid loud noises, and refrain from touching art installations.

Fees: General admission $15 for adults, $10 for children.
Operating Hours: 9 AM - 5 PM, daily.
Transportation: Accessible by DART, with ample parking available.

- Don’ts: No outside food allowed, avoid leaning on railings.
